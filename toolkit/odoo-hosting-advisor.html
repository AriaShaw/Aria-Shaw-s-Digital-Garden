What Could Go Wrong with Managed Hosting

Common Hidden Costs:
  • Bandwidth overages: Some providers charge extra above 1TB/month. For your user count, expect ~400GB/month.
  • Storage growth: Odoo databases grow 2-5GB/year per 10 users. Budget for storage upgrades by Year 2.
  • Support tiers: Basic support may not include phone/urgent response. Confirm SLA before signing.
Red Flags That Mean You Should Reconsider:
  • If you're planning heavy customization, managed hosts may restrict access.
  • If your budget is tight (<$300/month), VPS self-hosting might be necessary—but only with technical help.

"What If I Chose Differently?"

▶ What if I self-host on a VPS instead?

Lower cost, higher risk. You'd save ~$200/month but need to handle security patches, backups, monitoring, and troubleshooting. For your technical capacity, this adds 3-5 hours/month of work—and one mistake could cost you more than 2 years of savings. Best for: Teams with dedicated Linux expertise or very tight budgets willing to invest learning time.

▶ What if I use Odoo.sh (official SaaS)?

Maximum convenience, premium price. You'd pay ~$250/month more than managed hosting for the same user count. You get instant setup, automatic scaling, and direct Odoo support. Trade-off: Less control (can't install custom Linux packages), harder to migrate away (proprietary deployment structure). Best for: Teams prioritizing speed-to-launch over long-term cost optimization.
